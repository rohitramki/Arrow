Welcome to Arrow

Arrow is a free stock market tracking tool that allows users to keep track of their portfolio at up to date prices

Why Choose Arrow?

Unlike sites like Robinhood and Webull, Arrow allows you to track stock without using money. This allows you to paper trade stock risk free! And unlike other stock market tracking tools, Arrow is completely free to use!

Our Misson

At Arrow we believe that everyone should be able to track their investments regardless of their financial standing. For this reason, we provide people a dynamic portfolio tracker free of cost.
